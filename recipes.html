Smoke Break
Peated scotch, coffee, and bitter orange — a dark old-fashioned that lingers.
— Ingredients —
  • Monkey Shoulder Blended Malt Scotch 2 oz
  • Laphroaig 10 Islay Single Malt ½ oz
  • Coffee Liqueur State Line ¾ oz
  • Demerara Syrup 2:1 rich ¼ oz
  • Orange Bitters Regan's or Angostura 2 dashes
— Method —
Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Stir 30–40 seconds until well-chilled. Strain over a large cube into a double rocks glass. Express orange peel and rest on rim.
Glass Double Rocks
Ice Large Cube
Garnish Orange Peel
